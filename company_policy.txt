# Copilot / AI Auditor Enterprise Support Policy

1.  Greeting & Opening Policy
    - Agent must greet the customer politely and offer assistance.
2.  Empathy Policy
    - Agent must acknowledge customer issues with understanding and concern.
3.  Verification Policy
    - Agent must verify customer/order details before proceeding.
4.  Policy Compliance (Return/Refund Policy)
    - Agent must correctly apply and communicate company policies (e.g., 7-day return window).
5.  Transparency Policy
    - Agent must clearly explain available options and processes.
6.  Resolution Policy
    - Agent must take appropriate action to resolve the issue efficiently.
7.  Timeline Communication Policy
    - Agent must provide accurate timelines for resolution (refunds, pickups, etc.).
8.  Process Clarity Policy
    - Agent must explain next steps clearly (returns, pickup instructions).
9.  Professional Communication Policy
    - Agent must maintain polite, respectful, and clear communication throughout.
10. Closing Policy
    - Agent must close the conversation politely and offer further assistance.
