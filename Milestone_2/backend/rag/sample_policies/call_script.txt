APPROVED CALL SCRIPT — AI AUDITOR PLATFORM
Version 2.3 | March 2026
===========================================

OPENING SCRIPT
--------------
Agent: "Thank you for calling [Company] Support. My name is [Name],
        and I'm happy to help you today. Could I start with your full name please?"

[Wait for response]

Agent: "Thank you, [Customer Name]. Before I access your account,
        I need to verify your identity. Could you please provide your date of birth?"

[If verification fails]
Agent: "No problem — as an alternative, could you provide the PIN
        on your account or the last 4 digits of your registered phone number?"

EMPATHY + ISSUE DISCOVERY
---------------------------
Agent: "I'm sorry to hear you're experiencing [restate issue].
        I completely understand how [frustrating/inconvenient/concerning] that must be.
        Let me pull up your account and take a closer look."

[Paraphrase back:]
Agent: "Just to make sure I have this right — you're experiencing [X], 
        and it's been happening since [Y]. Is that correct?"

HOLD SCRIPT
-----------
Agent: "I'd like to place you on a brief hold while I look into this for you.
        It should only take a minute or two. Is that okay?"

[After hold:]
Agent: "Thank you so much for your patience. I appreciate you holding."

RESOLUTION DELIVERY
-------------------
Agent: "Great news — I've been able to [action taken]. Here's what I did:
        [Step 1], [Step 2]. This should resolve the issue you were experiencing."

[Walk through confirmation:]
Agent: "Does that resolve the issue for you today? Is there anything else
        I can help you with?"

COMPENSATION / GOODWILL SCRIPT (Authorized Tier)
--------------------------------------------------
Agent: "As an apology for the inconvenience, I'd like to apply a [amount] credit
        to your account. You'll see this reflected within 24-48 hours.
        I hope this helps and thank you for your patience."

ESCALATION SCRIPT
-----------------
Agent: "I want to make sure you get the best possible resolution, 
        so I'm going to connect you with one of our specialist team members.
        Your reference number for this interaction is [TICKET-ID].
        Please hold while I transfer you — this should take less than 60 seconds."

LEGAL THREAT SCRIPT (Mandatory)
--------------------------------
Agent: "I understand your concerns are serious. I'm going to connect you
        immediately with our customer relations team who is best equipped 
        to assist with this matter. Your reference number is [TICKET-ID].
        I'm transferring you now."

[DO NOT engage in discussion about legal matters. Transfer immediately.]

CLOSURE SCRIPT
--------------
Agent: "Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

[Wait for response]

Agent: "Wonderful. Thank you for contacting [Company] support, [Customer Name].
        I hope you have a great rest of your day. Goodbye!"

[NPS Survey prompt for resolved calls:]
Agent: "Before we close, you may receive a short survey about your experience today.
        We'd love your feedback if you have a moment. Thank you again!"

CHAT-SPECIFIC CLOSURE
----------------------
Agent: "It was a pleasure assisting you today! If you ever need further help,
        don't hesitate to reach out. Take care! 😊"
