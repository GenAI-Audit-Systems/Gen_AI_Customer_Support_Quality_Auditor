STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP) MANUAL — CUSTOMER SUPPORT
AI Auditor Platform | Version 3.1 | March 2026
===============================================================

SOP-1: GREETING & OPENING
---------------------------
1.1 Phone calls:
    "Thank you for calling [Company Name] support. My name is [Agent Name].
     How can I assist you today?"

1.2 Chat:
    "Hello! Welcome to [Company Name] support. I'm [Agent Name] and I'm here to help.
     How can I assist you today?"

1.3 Agents must greet within 3 seconds of connection. Delayed greeting is flagged.

1.4 Agents must introduce themselves by first name in every interaction.

SOP-2: ACTIVE LISTENING & PROBING
-----------------------------------
2.1 After the customer explains their issue, the agent must:
    - Paraphrase the issue to confirm understanding
    - Ask at least ONE clarifying question before proposing a solution

2.2 Empathy statement required for any complaint or frustration expressed:
    Examples:
    - "I completely understand how frustrating this must be."
    - "I'm sorry you're experiencing this — let me get this sorted for you."
    - "That sounds very inconvenient, and I appreciate your patience."

2.3 Agents must NOT rush to solutions before fully understanding the issue.
    "Resolution rushing" is a quality deduction.

SOP-3: HOLD PROCEDURE
----------------------
3.1 Before placing on hold: "May I place you on a brief hold while I look into this?"
    - Wait for customer confirmation before placing on hold.
3.2 Hold duration: maximum 2 minutes per hold cycle.
3.3 Check-back required if hold exceeds 90 seconds: 
    "Thank you for holding. I'm still working on this — just a moment more."
3.4 After hold: "Thank you for your patience!"

SOP-4: VERIFICATION PROCEDURE (SOP-4.2)
-----------------------------------------
4.1 Ask for:  Full name, then date of birth.
4.2 If DOB fails: offer PIN or last-4 of phone number.
4.3 Three failed attempts: lock session and escalate to Tier-2,
    issue reference ID to customer.
4.4 Never ask for full SSN, full card number, or password.

SOP-5: PROBLEM RESOLUTION STEPS
---------------------------------
5.1 Tier-1 resolution attempt: agent tries to resolve using available tools.
5.2 If unresolved in 5 minutes: escalate (do NOT keep customer waiting longer).
5.3 After resolution: walkthrough the solution step-by-step with the customer.
5.4 Confirm resolution: "Does that resolve your issue today?"

SOP-6: NEGATIVE FEEDBACK HANDLING
------------------------------------
6.1 Never be defensive when receiving negative feedback.
6.2 Acknowledge, apologize (without admitting fault for systemic issues), act.
    "I'm sorry to hear that. Let me make sure we address this right now."
6.3 If customer asks for a supervisor, honor within 90 seconds.
    Do NOT ask for a reason beyond "I'd like to connect you with my supervisor."

SOP-7: DOCUMENTATION & CASE NOTES
------------------------------------
7.1 Every interaction requires a case note with:
    - Issue category (billing / technical / account / general)
    - Resolution action taken
    - Follow-up required (yes/no)
    - Escalation flag (yes/no)
7.2 Case notes must be completed within 5 minutes of interaction close.

SOP-8: CHAT ETIQUETTE
-----------------------
8.1 Response time: within 30 seconds of each customer message.
8.2 Avoid typing in ALL CAPS (perceived as aggressive).
8.3 Use complete sentences. No abbreviations (e.g. "plz", "thx") in customer-facing messages.
8.4 Emoji allowed only in informal follow-up messages, never in complaints handling.
8.5 Multi-line responses: break into short paragraphs for readability.
