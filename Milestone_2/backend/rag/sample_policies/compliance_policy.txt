COMPLIANCE POLICY MANUAL — AI AUDITOR PLATFORM
Version 2.0 | Effective March 2026
================================================

SECTION 1: AGENT IDENTITY VERIFICATION (MANDATORY)
---------------------------------------------------
1.1 Agents MUST verify customer identity before accessing any account information.
    Required verification fields:
    - Full name (first + last)
    - Date of birth OR account PIN
    - Last 4 digits of registered phone number or email address

1.2 Failure to verify identity before sharing account information constitutes a
    CRITICAL compliance violation and must be flagged immediately.

1.3 Agents may NOT proceed with any account modification without completing
    the two-factor verification protocol described in SOP-4.2.

SECTION 2: DATA PRIVACY & GDPR COMPLIANCE
------------------------------------------
2.1 Agents must NEVER read back full credit card numbers, Social Security numbers,
    or national ID numbers over the phone or chat.

2.2 Customers have the right to request data deletion. Agents must:
    - Acknowledge the request within the same interaction
    - Escalate to the Data Privacy team within 24 hours
    - Provide a reference ticket number

2.3 Recording consent must be confirmed verbally at the start of every call:
    "This call may be recorded for quality and training purposes. Do you consent?"

2.4 Personal data shared by the customer must NOT be retained in chat logs 
    beyond what is operationally necessary. Agents must follow data minimization.

SECTION 3: PROHIBITED LANGUAGE & CONDUCT
-----------------------------------------
3.1 Agents must NEVER:
    - Use profanity or aggressive language
    - Interrupt a customer more than once
    - Make promises that cannot be fulfilled (e.g., guaranteeing refunds not approved)
    - Discuss competitor pricing or capabilities
    - Disclose internal system issues or outage details beyond what is approved

3.2 Agents must maintain a calm, professional tone regardless of customer behavior.

3.3 If a customer becomes verbally abusive, agents should issue one warning and
    then politely end the interaction. This is NOT a compliance violation.

SECTION 4: ESCALATION PROTOCOL
--------------------------------
4.1 The following situations REQUIRE immediate escalation:
    - Customer threatens legal action
    - Customer reports fraudulent account activity
    - Customer is experiencing a service outage affecting business operations
    - Customer is in emotional distress (e.g., bereavement, medical emergency context)

4.2 Escalation means transferring to a Tier-2 specialist or supervisor within 60 seconds.
    Agents must NOT place the customer on hold for more than 2 minutes before escalating.

4.3 Agents must document the escalation reason in the case notes before transferring.

SECTION 5: REFUND & COMPENSATION POLICY
-----------------------------------------
5.1 Agents are authorized to offer:
    - Goodwill credit up to $25 without manager approval
    - Refund for billing errors upon verification
    - Service credit for outage-related issues (max 30 days)

5.2 Any compensation exceeding authorized limits requires supervisor override code.

5.3 Agents must NEVER offer cash refunds via chat. All cash refund processing 
    goes through the finance portal with 5-7 business day processing time.

SECTION 6: CALL CLOSURE REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------
6.1 Every interaction MUST close with:
    - Confirmation that the issue is resolved (or escalated with ticket number)
    - Offer of further assistance ("Is there anything else I can help you with?")
    - Courtesy close ("Thank you for contacting us. Have a great day!")

6.2 Abrupt disconnection without a proper closure is a compliance violation.

6.3 NPS survey invitation must be offered at the end of every resolved interaction.
